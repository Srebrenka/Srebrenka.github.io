Advanced Methods Training & Continuing Professional Development

-   I have undertaken extensive continuing professional development in advanced quantitative methods, computational social science, open science, and data science. My training in social network analysis has included workshops and courses on temporal and dynamic network modelling, including Introduction to Temporal Exponential Random Graph Models (TERGMs) using statnet, SIENA longitudinal analysis of social networks, Advanced RSiena, Advanced RSiena and Relational Event Models, valued tie network modelling with statnet, moderation and mediation analysis in ERGMs using ergMargins, and research design workshops on network processes and configurations. I have also completed specialist training in multiplex, multimodal, and signed network analysis, including Multiplex Social Network Analysis with multip2, Multimodal Network Analysis with migraph, algebraic analysis and visualisation of multiple, signed and two-mode networks, and advanced metrics and diffusion modelling using manynet and migraph.

-   My broader computational and quantitative training has included empirically calibrated network simulations, agent-based models in social network analysis using NetLogo, multilevel modelling, multilevel modelling for egocentric network analysis, missing data analysis using the mice R package, and workshops in design and execution of group experiments. In data science and programming, I completed a Data Science Bootcamp in Machine Learning in Python and The Data Scientist’s Toolbox. Additional specialist training has included the Psychological Networks Winter School, Complex Networks: Theory, Methods and Applications II, Social Network Approaches for Behaviour Change, and training in EgoWeb 2.0 social network data collection software.

-   I am completing the Certificate in Learning, Teaching and Assessment in Higher Education (expected May 2026).
